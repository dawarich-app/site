# Dawarich vs Polarsteps

Polarsteps is a travel tracking app that automatically records your trips and lets you create visual travel journals with photos, notes, and maps. It's popular among backpackers, road trippers, and travel bloggers.

While Polarsteps focuses on **travel journaling and social sharing**, Dawarich focuses on **comprehensive location history, data ownership, and privacy**. This page compares both to help you decide.

## Key Differences

**Polarsteps is a travel journal.** It tracks your trips, adds photos and notes, and creates shareable stories. It's great for documenting vacations and sharing them with friends and family. It only tracks when you're on a trip.

**Dawarich is a location history platform.** It tracks your location continuously — not just trips — and gives you tools to analyze, visualize, and own years of location data. It's for people who want a complete record of where they've been.

## When to Choose Dawarich

- You want to track your location **every day**, not just on trips
- You care about **data privacy** and want to own your location data
- You want to **import Google Timeline** or other location exports
- You want **heatmaps** and detailed statistics over your full history
- You want a **web interface** for exploring data on a big screen
- You don't need social sharing or travel journaling features

## When to Choose Polarsteps

- You mainly want to track and share **specific trips** (vacations, road trips)
- You want to create **travel journals** with photos and notes
- You want to share trips with **friends and family** in real-time
- You want **printed travel books** from your trips
- You don't need continuous daily location tracking
